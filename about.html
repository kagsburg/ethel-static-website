About Us

Welcome to Ethel Kindergarten

Ethel Preschool and daycare is located in the heart of Kampala city in a surburb called Kanyanya in Uganda. It was founded in January 2022 by Ms. Nassuna Ntabadde Miriam. Ethel is a german word meaning noble. We chose it as we aim at providing services to achieve fine personal qualities and high moral standards amongst both our staff and students.

Ethel preschool and daycare began as an idea conceived by Ms. Nassuna Ntabadde Miriam in 2021. She had a vision of providing a safe, secure and loving environment for children to learn and grow. She started with a few children in her during the covid pandemic lockdown period of 2020-2021. During this perid parents were stranded with their children who were rendered idle and untamed.

There was need for a caretaker to look after the children ages(1-7) of the parents who were working in the community. Miriam decided to dedicate her time to provide quality healthy and safe child care hence the birth of our wonderful School when the lockdown was lifted, the school was registered and started operating in January 2022.

We started with a total of 26 children and 4 members of staff. At Ethel preschool and daycare we believe that every child is unique and special. We plan for the development of our learners according to their age group i.e:

  • Play group (1-3 years)
  • Baby Class (3-4 years)
  • Kindergarteners (4-5 years)
  • Pre-primary (5-6 years)
