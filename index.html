Activities

Kids Activities

Full Day Session

Children that stay at school under the fullday programme are taken well car of as they interact through various activities. These activities are majorly play based to ensure the children feel safe and happy.

Table/Floor Toys

These toys are intended to develop and control small motor skills, improve concentration, encourage completion of tasks and enhance self-esteem. The toys also provide interpersonal skills such as sharing, taking turns and cooperation among children.

Outdoor Games

These games aid with brain development where the children invent new games, explore their surroundings and become more independent. For example Tag, Hide and Seek, Hopscotch, Jump Rope, Sack races, etc.

Play Area

We have a spacious environment which gives our children the freedom to explore and play. The play area is well equipped with toys and equipment that are age appropriate. This helps them improve flexibility and balance, develop hand-eye coordination, strengthen their muscles and enhance instinct.
